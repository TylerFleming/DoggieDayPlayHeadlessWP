About Us

Doggie Day Play is a premium dog daycare and hotel serving the Oak Park, River Forest and surrounding area. We are an all-suite facility with large play yards and an attentive staff that will make your dog feel happy and loved. We are obsessed with cleanliness and making your dog feel loved. We welcome you to visit us and see how we can provide an experience that exceeds your expectations.

Meet Our Staff

We have the best staff. We are very proud of the people that are on hand to care for your family member. Everyone that works at Doggie Day Play has been trained extensively in the nature of dog behavior and body language. Our staff manages the play groups to create groups that will compliment each other’s common characteristics. We do not just put all big dogs together just because they’re big. We learn the personality of our guests to find the right mix of play friends and to minimize the chance of skirmishes.
